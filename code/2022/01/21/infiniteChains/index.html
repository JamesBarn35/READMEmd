But each link in a monadic chain comes at a cost in terms of complexity. They each increase a given function's cyclomatic complexity, furthering the likelihood of introducing bugs. Long monadic chains are also indicative that a function isn't adhering to the single responsibility rule. We want to use chaining to improve our separation of concerns and simplify the code, not make things worse.
